Hand surgery

  1. Brief didactic teaching / group discussion session (15-20 mins)

  2. Discuss simple x-ray film interpretation technique with Colles / Smith’s fractures as examples

  3. History and examination

  4. Wrist fracture / falls history if appropriate patient discovered

  5. Otherwise facilitate multiple members of the group performing a formal hand and wrist examination
