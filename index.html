Journal Club

Journal club is a resource I have put together in preparation for my own learning, as well as for BPT examinations. The goal is to practice research appraisal, and understand the most current literature. Through recurrent exposure to new evidence, the goal is to learn how to incorporate new knowledge into clinical practice in an effective manner.
