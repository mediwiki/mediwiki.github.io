Asthma Action Plan

Order of strength (strong  weak): ciclesonide, beclomethasone, fluticasone, budenoside

Scenario: 5M needs Ventolin for wheeze everyday, wakes up 2x/week, wheeze and cough during exercise – moderate persistent

Scenario: 8F flare every 4 weeks needs regular Ventolin for a few days, well in between, exercise sx, FEV1 82% predicted – frequent intermittent

Example weaning plan

  • Ventolin: 6 puffs 4hrly → 6hrly → 8hrly → 4 puffs BD → 2 puffs BD
  • Pred: 3ml=15mg → 3ml=15mg → stop by Day 3
