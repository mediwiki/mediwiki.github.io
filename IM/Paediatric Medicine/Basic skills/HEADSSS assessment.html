HEADSSS

Components of the HEADSSS screen

  1. Home
  2. Education, Eating
  3. Activities
  4. Drugs
  5. Sexuality

  7. Safety

Introduction to patient and family

  • Tell patient and family the structure of how the consultation will work
    • Speak together to understand the presentation
    • Speak to child 1 on 1 to understand social issues, etc.
  • Discuss confidentiality

PRO-FORMA FOR HEADSSSS SCREEN

Confidentiality

  • Hurting yourself, hurting others, someone else hurting you, committing a crime

Home

  1. who do you life with (anyone else in household?)
  2. How do you get along with your family members
  3. Ask where the other family members are, how often do you see your family members
  4. where do you live
  5. Who do you go to if you need help with something
  6. have you ever thought about running away
  7. do you ever call home when you're late
  8. Try to identify which adult members they can rely on if something was really worrying

Eating

  1. happy with your weight?
  2. spend time thinking about weight or what you eat?
  3. anyone else worried about your weight or eating habits?

Education and employment

  1. do you go to school
  2. what school do you go to
  3. what year are you in
  4. do you like school?
  5. what subjects are you good at?
  6. What subjects are you not so good at?
  7. Have your grades changed at all lately
  8. Why?
  9. How much school have you missed in the last 3 months?
  10. how do you get along with the teachers and other students?
  11. do you have a plan for when you leave school
  12. have you got a job?
  13. many young people experience bullying at school and at work. is this something that you've experienced?
  14. who do you go to if you need help with a problem?
  15. working, hours spent working, why come home late

Activities

  1. who do you hang out with?
  2. do you do any other physical activities?
  3. What about other things in your spare time? Hobbies?
  4. What sort of things do you like to do with your friends?
  5. how often do you phone, text, or use the computer for chatting
  6. how much tv do you watch?
  7. are you in any groups or clubs
  8. how do you think other people would describe you?
  9. how much time do you spend on the internet, gaming, and using facebook?

Drugs

  • normalise drug taking, smoking, alcohol, re-iterate confidentiality
  • do and of your friends do that?
  • quantify alcohol intake, what context this occurs in
  • do your parents know about your alcohol consumption
  • How about yourself?
  • have you tried smoking
  • have you tried any other drugs
  • have you ever been really drunk, blacked out, or done things that you've regretted when you're drunk or stoned?
  • reiterate confidentality

Sexuality

  • at your age a lot of people are experimenting with sex
  • Do you have a boyfriend or girlfriend?
  • Have you had any sexual encounters?
  • Do you ever worry about who you're attracted to?
  • have you had sex education?
  • what do you know about contraception and sexually transmitted infections?
  • what do you understand about safe sex?
  • Has anyone ever touched you in a way that made you feel uncomfortable?
  • has anyone ever forced you to have sex?

Suicide and Safety

  1. are you a worrier
  2. are you feeling tearful and sad? How often?
  3. Do you worry about things or people
  4. is there anyone you can talk to about this
  5. some people who feel really down often feel like hurting themselves or even killing themselves. Have you ever felt like this?
  6. Have you ever tried to hurt yourself? What stopped you?
  7. do you feel the same way now?
  8. do you have a plan?
  9. have you ever ridden in a car with a driver who was drunk or high?
  10. have you ever been in a physical fight?
  11. have you been in trouble with the police or courts?
  12. have you ever felt the need to carry a weapon for protection?
  13. have you experienced online bullying?
  14. have you ever been asked for personal details over the internet or be made to feel uncomfortable?
