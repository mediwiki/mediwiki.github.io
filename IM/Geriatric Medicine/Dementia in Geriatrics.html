Dementia as a geriatric syndrome

Background

  • acquired, persistent and progressive impairment of intellectual function
  • compromise of 1+ cognitive domains
  • prevalence doubles every 5 years in older population
    • 30-50% in >= 85 year olds
  • Preventative factors
    • higher education levels
    • control of CVD risk factors
  • Causes
    • Alzheimers disease (2/3rds)
    • Vascular dementia
    • Lewy body dementia
  • Associations
    • Depression - MDD in 20-50%
    • Delirium

DSM5 definition

  • Complex attention
    • easily distracted, difficulty performing calculations
  • Executive function
    • poor abstraction, mental flexibility, planning, judgement
  • Learning and memory
    • recall, recount
  • Language
    • word finding and object naming
  • Percetual motor function
    • navigation, copying image
  • Social cognition
    • personality, social cues
  • Additionally requires functional decline to loss independence in IADLs
