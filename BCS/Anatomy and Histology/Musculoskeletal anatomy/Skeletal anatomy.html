Skeletal anatomy

Background

  • Skeleton made of bone (see Bone histology)
  • Composed of two subsets
    • Axial skeleton
      • down the vertical axis, including skull, vertebral column, ribs and sternum
    • Appendicular skeleton
      • Upper limb
        • clavicle, scapula
        • humerus
        • ulna, radius
        • carpals, metacarpals
        • phalanges
      • Lower limb
        • pelvis
        • femur, patella
        • tibia, fibula
        • tarsals, metatarsals, phalanges

Anatomy of bones

Types of bones

  1. Long bones
    1. Humerus, radius, metacarpals, phalanges
  2. Short bones
    1. Carpal, tarsal bones
  3. Flat bones
    1. Cranial vault bones, sternum
  4. Irregular bones
    1. Scapula, vertebral bones, facial bones
  5. Sesamoid bones
    1. Patellar
